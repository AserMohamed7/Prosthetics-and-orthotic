Custom Orthotic

Spinal Orthotics

Our spinal orthotics are designed to support and stabilize the spine, providing relief and enhancing mobility for individuals with spinal conditions. Customized to fit each patient's needs, these devices offer comfort and effectiveness.

Cranial Orthotics

We offer cranial orthotics that are specially designed to correct and support the shape of an infant's head. These custom-made helmets are crafted with care, ensuring the best possible fit and comfort for your child.

Upper Extremity Orthotics

Our upper extremity orthotics provide support and stability to the arms, wrists, and hands. These devices are designed to aid in recovery, reduce pain, and improve function for those with injuries or conditions affecting the upper limbs.

Lower Extremity Orthotics

Our lower extremity orthotics are crafted to enhance mobility and support for the legs and feet. Whether you need support for walking, running, or everyday activities, our orthotics are tailored to meet your specific needs.

Diabetic Foot Care

Our diabetic foot care services focus on protecting and managing the health of your feet. With custom orthotics and preventive care, we help reduce the risk of complications and ensure optimal foot health for individuals with diabetes.
