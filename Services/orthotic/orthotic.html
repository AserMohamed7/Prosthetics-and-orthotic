Custom Orthotic

Spinal Orthotics

Our spinal orthotics are designed to support and stabilize the spine, providing relief and enhancing mobility for individuals with spinal conditions. Customized to fit each patient's needs, these devices offer comfort and effectiveness.

Ankle Foot Orthosis

We offer cranial orthotics that are specially designed to correct and support the shape of an infant's head. These custom-made helmets are crafted with care, ensuring the best possible fit and comfort for your child.

KAFO for Polio Patients

A KAFO device tailored for polio patients, designed to support the entire leg, stabilizing both the knee and ankle, helping patients walk with greater stability and independence.

Hand splints

Hand splints designed to stabilize the wrist and hand after Neurological injuries or surgeries, providing the necessary support to speed up recovery and improve functionality.

Boston Brace

The Boston brace is an orthotic device designed to stabilize the entire spine, providing firm and continuous support through its strong, lightweight design. It is easily worn under clothing and is ideal for daily compliance.

Cheneau Brace

The Cheneau brace is a 3D orthotic device designed to correct spinal deformities with a high level of precision. It provides targeted support and correction through its custom-fit design, helping to manage and improve spinal curvature effectively. The brace's structured approach ensures comprehensive support and adjustment, tailored to each individual’s needs.
