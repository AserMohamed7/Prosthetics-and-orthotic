Upper Limb Prosthetics

Below-knee prosthetics are designed for individuals who have lost part of their leg below the knee. Thanks to precise design, our prosthetics provide the perfect balance of strength and flexibility, restoring your natural movement and ensuring your comfort and independence.

Above-knee and knee-disarticulation prosthetics

Myoelectric Prosthesis

Myoelectric prosthetics use advanced technology to provide intuitive control and improved functionality. Equipped with sensors that detect electrical signals from the user's muscles, these prosthetics enable natural, responsive movements of the hand and fingers. They offer a high level of precision and ease in performing daily tasks, combining modern technology with a comfortable, custom-fit design.

Silicone Prosthetics

Silicone prosthetics are designed for those seeking high-level aesthetics and realism. Crafted with meticulous attention to detail, these prosthetics closely mimic the appearance of natural skin, down to the smallest details like texture, color, and even veins. They provide a seamless, lifelike look, offering wearers enhanced confidence and comfort.

Advanced Prosthetics

Advanced prosthetics feature smart chips supported by artificial intelligence that analyze movement patterns and assist in optimizing mobility. These innovative prosthetics adjust in real-time to enhance functional performance and ensure seamless integration into daily activities. We are among the few providers in Egypt capable of offering and fitting this cutting-edge technology
